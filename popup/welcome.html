Welcome to iFrame Ad Blocker!

How It Works

iFrame Ad Blocker specifically targets and blocks ads that are delivered through iFrames on websites. iFrames are containers that websites use to embed external content - often advertisements.

Important: This blocker is designed for iframe-based ads, which are common in video players, social media embeds, and many banner ads. It doesn't block all types of ads (like native ads or script-injected ads).

Features

Blocks ads in video players
Stops iframe-based banner advertisements
Enhances privacy by limiting tracking in embedded frames
Works automatically on all websites
Simple on/off toggle when needed

Getting Started

The blocker is already active and working in the background. You'll notice fewer iframe-based ads as you browse.

If you need to temporarily disable the blocker, click the extension icon in your toolbar and use the toggle switch.
